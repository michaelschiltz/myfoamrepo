# The ascent narrative is the Jensen error as historiography

The project's framework cuts directly against the ascent narrative, which is why the word fits. If multiplicative dynamics and absorbing barriers are the governing facts, then "innovation" frequently does *not* reduce ruin risk but **relocates and conceals** it — entity-shielding lets the system survive precisely by exporting absorption onto counterparties, creditors, or the sovereign.

The ascent narrative is Whiggish exactly because it reads *survivorship as improvement*, which is the [[Jensen gap|Jensen's-inequality error]] dressed as historiography: it averages over the survivors and calls the result progress. The instruments that persisted are read as more efficient because the ones that hit the barrier were removed from the sample and from the story. Progress is the shape left behind when you delete the ruined.
