# Jensen gap

The wedge between the average of a function and the function of the average under convexity/concavity — the formal residue that a monopoly contract collects and launders through law. The coercion forcing a Bandanese grower to sell only to the VOC at the VOC's price is a Jensen gap collected through a lawful document; the coercion is invisible in the ledger precisely because it has been laundered through the contract.
