# Entity-shielding

Legal partition insulating an entity's assets and liabilities from those of its members (and vice versa) — the channel through which [[Absorbing barrier]] dynamics get altered. It converts an individual's multiplicative, time-series risk into pooled, cross-sectional risk, making ensemble reasoning *locally* valid for the shielded agent.

Facets surfacing so far, to keep distinct:
- **Asset-partitioning** — the charter shields a commercial corpus (VOC; the Antwerp *naties*' *alleenrecht* held as tradable shares).
- **Identity-wrapper** — the corporate form as a flag-of-convenience masking a foreign principal (the *nación flamenca*).
- **Perpetual-monopoly** — a licensed franchise held as property, outliving its members (*naties*; *kabu-nakama*; the *ie*).
