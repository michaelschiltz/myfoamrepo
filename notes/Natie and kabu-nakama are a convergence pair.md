# Natie and kabu-nakama are a convergence pair

The *natie* maps onto the Japanese material better than the *kō* did, and along a precise axis. The *alleenrecht*-held-as-shares is a near-exact structural analogue of the **kabu** of the *kabu-nakama* — a licensed monopoly right that is itself corporate property and tradable. Natie ≈ *kabu-nakama*; the *natiebaas*'s share ≈ *kabu*; the *ton'ya / toiya* (問屋) wholesale-transport agents are the functional cousins of the cargo *naties*.

This is a convergence pair on the *monopoly-franchise* facet as tight as the *avería* / *tōgin* pair is on the *risk-mutualisation* facet — two independent traditions arriving at monopoly-right-as-tradable-property. It is evidence for the comparative spine precisely because the two forms are not plausibly connected by transmission (cf. [[Transmission versus independent recurrence is the load-bearing test]]).
