# Refusals are observations of the filter not inferences from survivors

A refused form is worth more than an adopted one, and for a reason deeper than that refusals are rarer.

Adoption is overdetermined — a form that spreads or recurs is consistent with a dozen stories (useful, copied, imposed). A **refusal** — a form *available and declined*, as the sea loan was known through Mediterranean contact and refused — locates the boundary of the selective filter. Adoptions sample from the filter; refusals reveal its shape. The refusal is the dog that didn't bark: the negative observation that identifies the active criterion.

The decisive point answers a worry the vault already holds. [[Time-average optimization is a survivorship property not an intention]] concedes that persistence alone may be mere survivorship — we see only survivors, so of course they look adapted, and the inference threatens circularity. A refusal breaks the circle, because it is an observation of the **selection event itself**, not of its output: it shows the filter acting, rejecting the fragile form while admitting the robust one. That is what upgrades the story from "these happened to survive" to "there was a criterion doing the selecting." Adoptions cannot distinguish selection from drift or lock-in; refusals can. Refusals are therefore what convert the persistence claim from survivorship bias into demonstrated selection.

The guard against overclaiming: a refusal is an argument from silence unless **availability** is established — that the form was genuinely within reach and declined, not absent for want of contact nor adopted-but-undocumented. Establishing availability is a minimal contact-and-transmission claim, so provenance re-enters here — not as priority but as the availability history that licenses calling an absence a refusal. The sea loan case is strong precisely because availability (Mediterranean entanglement) and an active mechanism (*riba*, *gharar*) can both be shown. Weaker "refusals" are evidence-gaps dressed as decisions, and a referee will test each at that joint. See [[Split provenance into priority and independence]].
