# The ortoq is the discriminating instrument

The discriminating instrument is the Mongol–Yuan **ortoq**, a securely identified pooling form that demonstrably travelled — and, on specialist assessment, a genuine steppe development rather than a re-export of the Islamic form. A *documented* case of transmission is what licenses an independent-origins reading elsewhere, precisely because it proves the project can *tell the two apart*: with the signature of real diffusion in hand, convergence that lacks that signature can be read as independent.

The Sogdian network, by contrast, enters only as evidence that transmission was *physically possible* — its own commercial-legal record is too thin, and too Near-Eastern-inflected, to stand as an independent legal tradition. So the *ortoq* is the bridge that carries the whole comparative argument: it is the calibrated instance against which the East Asia / West comparison is read.
