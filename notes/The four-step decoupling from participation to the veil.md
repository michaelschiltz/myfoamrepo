# The four-step decoupling from participation to the veil

The move from participant to shielded shareholder is four steps, not one, and collapsing them is the compression a referee will catch.

1. **Price the risk.**
2. **Pool it** — step off the operational trajectory into the rentier position.
3. **Limited liability** — sever the personal absorbing barrier from the venture's.
4. **Tradeable shares and the management of other people's money** — decouple decision from consequence entirely.

The sea-loan lender takes only steps 1–2. He still holds *aggregate* skin in the game — his own capital, exposed to the systematic tail. He is the *embryo* of the shareholder, not the shareholder. To say "the price removed his skin in the game" attributes to a fourteenth-century maritime lender a decoupling that only the joint-stock veil completes.

The long arc is one trajectory: **sea loan → marine insurance → the joint-stock veil**, each move decoupling payoff further from participation. [[Islamic doctrine refuses risk-commodification at step one]] plants its flag against step 1 and refuses it.
