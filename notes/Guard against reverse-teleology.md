# Guard against reverse-teleology

The mirror charge is the one a good reader will bring: anti-Whiggism can install its own teleology. If the narrative reads a thousand years as recurring absorption and shielding, a referee will ask whether you have merely *inverted the arrow* — swapped a progress teleology for a declinist or eternal-recurrence one, imposing ergodicity economics anachronistically on actors who had no such concepts.

Butterfield's own later work is the hedge: he was not against *all* structure in narrative, only against the retrospective smuggling of the outcome into the account of the process. So the defense must be that ergodicity is an *analytical lens on the dynamics*, not a claim about what past actors believed or intended — otherwise you commit the present-centeredness you are indicting. This is the same discipline as [[Time-average optimization is a survivorship property not an intention]]: structure in the analysis, not intention in the actors.
